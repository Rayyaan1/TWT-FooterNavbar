Tarbaj Waqf Trust is a registered Charitable Trust in Kenya for the benefit and general welfare of the local Muslims. It is duly registered under the (Trustees) perpetual succession Act (Cap 164) and the Trustees Act (Cap 167) of the laws of Kenya. In particular, it operates to serve the educational needs of the residents constituency of Tarbaj, Wajir County, NE Kenya, with a land mass of over 9,650kmÂ².
